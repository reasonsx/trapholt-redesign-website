ABOUT TRAPHOLT

Welcome to Trapholt – a cultural haven nestled in the heart of Denmark, where art and design come to life in a harmonious blend of past and present. Trapholt is not just a museum; it's an immersive experience that celebrates creativity, innovation, and the rich tapestry of Danish cultural heritage.


What makes Trapholt truly special is our commitment to inclusivity. We believe that art is for everyone, and to foster a love for creativity from an early age, we extend a warm welcome to children under the age of 18, who can access Trapholt for free. We invite families to come together and discover the wonders of our exhibitions, creating lasting memories and inspiring the next generation of art enthusiasts.


Trapholt is not just a museum, it's a place where imaginations soar, curiosity thrives, and the joy of discovery knows no bounds. Whether you're a seasoned art connoisseur or a first-time visitor, our doors are open, and a world of artistic wonder awaits you at Trapholt.

FAMILIES AT TRAPHOLT

At Trapholt, we believe that art is not just for connoisseurs—it's for families, too. Nestled in the heart of creativity, Trapholt is a heaven for art enthusiasts of all ages. Whether you're a seasoned art lover or introducing your little ones to the wonders of creativity, Trapholt promises a unique and family-friendly experience.


Our commitment to family-friendliness sets us apart. Trapholt is not just an art destination; it's a place where families can create lasting memories together. We understand the importance of catering to all ages, and that's why we've designed our space to be welcoming to families with children.


At our café, your little ones are engaged in the specially crafted Kids Corner. Filled with games, books, and creative activities, the Kids Corner ensures that even the youngest visitors have a fantastic time while you unwind.


Join us at Trapholt, where families connect, create, and cherish art together.
